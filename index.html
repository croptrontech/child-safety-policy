Lokmangal Setu - Child Safety Standards

Our Commitment to Child Safety

Lokmangal Setu is committed to providing a safe environment for all users and has zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) and child sexual abuse material (CSAM).

Prevention Measures

• Proactive content moderation and monitoring

• User reporting mechanisms for inappropriate content

• Age verification processes where applicable

• Regular safety audits and updates

Reporting Mechanism

Users can report child safety concerns directly within the app. All reports are reviewed immediately and appropriate action is taken.

Legal Compliance

We comply with all relevant child safety laws and report violations to appropriate regional and national authorities as required.

Response Actions

Violations result in immediate account suspension, content removal, and reporting to law enforcement when required.
